## 🧲 High-Frequency Magnetic Components

### 20 kVA Amorphous-Core Transformer
Designed, developed, and tested 20 kVA high-frequency amorphous-core transformers operating at 1 kHz fundamental frequency for a 50 kW EV charger. Optimized for low core loss, reduced leakage inductance, high efficiency, and improved thermal performance.

### 1 mH, 25 A Amorphous-Core Inductor
Designed, developed, and tested 1 mH, 25 A amorphous-core inductors operating at 1 kHz fundamental frequency. Optimized for low copper loss, low core loss, thermal stability, and continuous-load operation.
